# HYPERCAT — Hyperrealistic Feline Baseline

```
230 bones : 500 muscles : 60k hairs : 64kHz hearing : 25Hz purr
```

## Purpose

A single, verifiable, parametric model of *Felis catus* that serves as the hyperrealism baseline for all animal models in the Aetherion-Continuum engine. Every measurement is sourced from peer-reviewed biology. Every parameter is a vinculum bar — change one and the realism degrades measurably.

## Skeleton

- **230–250 bones** total (varies with tail + polydactyly)
- **7 cervical, 13 thoracic, 7 lumbar, 3 sacral, 18–23 caudal**
- Floating clavicle (12 mm sliver) — scapula slides 25 mm across ribcage
- Unfused radius/ulna — 140° pronation/supination
- Intervertebral disc thickness = 25% of vertebral body height
- Aerial righting reflex: 0.3 s, two independently rotating body halves, tail counterbalance

## Muscle & Locomotion

- **80% Type IIx** fast-twitch fibers with high mitochondrial density
- **300 W/kg** peak muscle power — among highest in mammals
- **5.5× body weight** takeoff force, 30 km/h sprint over 15 m
- Tendons contribute **30% of leap power** via elastic recoil
- Spine adds **20% stride length** through flexion/extension

## Fur

- **60,000 hairs/in²** (long-haired breeds)
- Guard hairs coated in sebum lipid layer
- Undercoat: fine crimped trapped-air insulation
- **Turing reaction-diffusion** for tabby patterns
- **X-inactivation mosaicism** for calico/tortoiseshell
- **Temperature-sensitive tyrosinase** for colorpoint (Siamese)

## Vision

- **Corneal f-number ~1.0** (fully dilated slit pupil)
- **Rod:cone ratio 25:1**, rod density 460,000/mm²
- **Tapetum lucidum**: 15–20 tiers riboflavin/zinc-cysteine crystals
- **+45% light sensitivity** gain, −12.5% acuity cost from scatter
- Dichromatic (deuteranope-equivalent), S-cone 450 nm, M/L-cone 553 nm
- **55 Hz flicker fusion** — tracks prey motion invisible to humans
- **130° binocular overlap**, stereopsis to 15 mm at 1 m

## Audition

- **3 cochlear turns**, 10,000 outer hair cells, 3,500 inner
- **64 kHz** upper limit at birth, adult range to 60 kHz
- **32 pinna muscles**, independent 180° rotation
- Spectral notch at ~10 kHz for direction cue
- Sound localization to **5° accuracy** — 5× better than human

## Olfaction

- **20 cm²** olfactory epithelium (human: 5 cm²)
- **200 million** receptor neurons, ~1,000 receptor types
- VNO with ~30 functional V1R genes
- Felinine synthesis: glutathione + methionine in kidney
- Flehmen response: vascular VNO pump

## Claws

- Deep digital flexor for protraction, elastic dorsal ligament for passive retraction
- Keratin sheath shed every ~60 days
- Scratching = maintenance + interdigital semiochemical deposition

## Vocalization

- **Purr: 25 Hz** — nucleus ambiguous brainstem oscillator
- Purr frequency coincides with **osteoblast stimulation range** (20–50 Hz)
- **Meow: 400–1,200 Hz** fundamental, learned neotenic behavior
- Solicitation purr mimics **infant cry (300–600 Hz)** to trigger human caregiving

## Domestication

- Diverged from *Felis lybica* ~10,000 years ago (Fertile Crescent)
- Self-selected for tameness in grain store commensal niche
- Neural crest migration deficits explain floppy ears, coat changes, reduced brain size
- Matrilineal colony structure with female philopatry

## Metabolism

- Obligate carnivore: cannot synthesize taurine, arginine, vitamin A, vitamin D3, arachidonic acid
- Tas1r2 sweet receptor pseudogenized — no fructose metabolism
- Single arginine-free meal → hyperammonemia → fatal within hours
